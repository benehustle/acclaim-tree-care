Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Acclaim Tree Care & Services (we, us) respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you interact with us from Brisbane, Ipswich, Logan and surrounding areas, and how we use it.

Information we collect

When you phone us on (07) 3160 5668, we note your name, address or job site, contact number and details of the tree work discussed. When you email info@acclaimtreecare.com.au, we retain your message and email address.

The quote form on this website collects your name, email, phone, selected service and message. Submitting the form does not store data on a server — it is a front-end demonstration only. In production you should connect the form to your email or CRM and update this policy accordingly.

How we use information

We use contact details to respond to quote requests, schedule site visits, carry out tree work and invoice for services. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Cookies and analytics

This static website does not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels or analytics scripts. If you add those tools later, publish an updated policy and consent approach.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information held in our business systems. No online transmission is completely secure; phone and email carry their own risks.

Access and correction

You may request access to personal information we hold about you, or ask us to correct it, by contacting us using the phone number or email above.

Complaints

If you believe we have mishandled your personal information, contact us first. You may also complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (oaic.gov.au).
