Who we are

Acclaim Tree Care & Services is a family-owned and operated business with over 10 years of experience serving Brisbane and surrounding areas. We are fully qualified arborists dedicated to delivering high-quality tree care, from complex removals to delicate pruning. Unlike operators who only cut and run, we prioritise the long-term health of your trees and the safety of your property.

Our team is fully insured, equipped with modern machinery, and we stand by our Full Site Clean-Up promise — your yard is left spotless after every job. We work with homeowners who want honest recommendations, commercial property managers who need reliable scheduling, and civil and government bodies that require documented, low-risk work on public land and road reserves.

Whether you are in Wishart, out towards Logan or west toward Ipswich, we plan each job around access, neighbour boundaries, overhead services and council expectations. If you are unsure what your tree needs, we will walk you through the options in plain English and quote clearly before we start.

What guides our work

Practical standards we apply on every site — from a single backyard gum to a full block clear.

Safety first

Rigging, exclusion zones and communication with everyone on site — including tenants and neighbours — come before speed.

Tree health

We prune with purpose: structure, clearance and storm resilience, not unnecessary lopping that weakens the canopy.

Respect for your place

We protect lawns, driveways and gardens where we can, and we remove chip and timber so you are not left with a mess.
